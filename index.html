Hi, I'm Ian. I'm an Iowan programmer with an ambition for software design and user interaction. I'm currently finishing out my B.S. degree in Computer Science at the University of Iowa, and I'm looking for employment in the Midwest shortly after graduating in winter of 2019.


This page is a small hub for everything you need to know about me, but if you have any questions for me (professional or otherwise), please feel free to send me an email at iangradert@gmail.com.


I greatly enjoy various forms of media; jazz and metal, shooter games and puzzle games, YouTube skits, 70s/80s cinema, and more. I have monthly features under the Monthlies section talking about my top picks for games and music. These posts detail why I'm enjoying that particular work and why you should too. I'm also still rapidly growing as a programmer and software developer - learning new tricks, refining my style, and adopting new practices. Posts about programming will show up more in the posts section, but if they're project-related, they'll also be in the Projects section.


I currently have one project in active development, and several others on the sketchboard, along with a plethora of other ideas that will likely never come to fruition. My current project is inspired by the likes of the old DOS games NetHack and Rogue, and draws heavily on fantasy elements from Dungeons & Dragons. Currently, it's titled Catacombs of Halfwind Keep.
