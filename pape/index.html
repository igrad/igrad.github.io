About Pape Productions

This is Curtis. Curtis fancies himself something of a cheese conisseur. His cheese-tasting abilities have been recognized with dozens of medals from across the fifty nifty, though he has yet to make his big break into the international cheese-tasting scene to compete with the big dogs. He also treasures his Persian cat, Goddard, whom he regularly brings with to his cheese tasting events for emotional support.
